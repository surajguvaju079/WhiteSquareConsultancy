Environmental Science

This program focuses on understanding and addressing environmental challenges through scientific research and practical solutions. Students explore topics like ecology, climate change, pollution, and sustainable development, preparing for careers in environmental management, conservation, or policy-making.

Hospitality and Tourism Management

This program emphasizes the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the global hospitality and tourism industries. Students explore topics such as hotel management, event planning, travel operations, and customer service, preparing for careers in hotels, resorts, event management, and tourism enterprises.

Information Technology

This program focuses on the development, implementation, and management of technology solutions. Students learn about software development, networking, cybersecurity, and IT infrastructure, equipping them for careers in software engineering, system administration, or IT consulting.
